Tasty Affair

Amix of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. A typical vegetarian meal comprises rice, rotis, dal fry, soup, and a vegetable preparation. The menu changes daily and non-veg options include dishes like chicken curry, pulao and more. Ideally place your order a day prior, or for dinner meals latest by 2PM on that day.

Meal: Lunch and dinner

  • Price: Rs 150 for a vegetarian meal and Rs 200 for a non-vegetarian meal
  • Delivery: Fatema and her husband undertake deliveries themselves around South Mumbai

The Harmony Kitchen

The lunch menu is usually fixed comprising one type of dal, like dal fry or dal pakwan, rotis, subzi, rice and a salad, while for dinner customers can request variations or something of their choice.
  • Price: Rs 175 for a lunch meal, while dinner varies depending on what you choose
  • Delivery: Free of cost in and around Lokhandwala and Kandivali. The kitchen also uses services like WeFast

Mama J's Kitchen

Breakfast options include dishes such as poha, upma with sheera, uttapams and more, while lunch and dinner options can be vegetarian or non-vegetarian and includes a dry and wet sabzi, dal, rice and chapatis with options like mix vegetable korma or sambar. Non-vegetarian options include chicken curry or fish fry, among others. You need to preferably place your order a day prior. Meal: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner

  • Rs 100 for a vegetarian breakfast meal,
  • Rs 200 for vegetarian lunch/dinne
  • Rs 250 for non-vegetarian lunch/dinner
Delivery: Between Mira Road and Malad via services like WeFast

Mom & Company

offering simple, home-cooked vegetarian meals for lunch and dinner. Spice Box
While this is a subscription meal service provider and not a home kitchen, if you’re looking for healthy, home-style meals, Spice Box offers a weekly subscription service providing vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals for lunch or dinner. A standard vegetarian meal will get you a vegetable preparation, dal, rice, rotis, raita, salad and dessert or you can also opt for a mini-meal which comes with one vegetarian dish, dal and rotis. You can even opt for a weekly plan alternating between veg and non-veg meals on different days.
  • Meal: Lunch and dinner
  • Price: Rs 120 for a standard vegetarian meal and Rs 110 for a mini-meal for a weekly plan
  • Delivery: Across Mumbai at a charge of Rs 35

Salt n Pepper

Harshada and Meet Pandya are cooking out of their home kitchen in Kandivali, offering vegetarian lunch and dinner meals for patients in quarantine. Lunch usually comprises a mix of dal, rice, rotis, and a sabzi while dinner offers variations such as pav bhaji, chole and the like. The meals are lightly spiced and cooked in keeping with requirements for COVID patients. You need to place orders for lunch the evening before while dinner orders can be taken latest by morning of the same day.

Meal: Lunch and dinner

  • Price: Rs 120 for a meal
  • Delivery: Between Malad and Borivali. Currently, these are being undertaken by the family themselves

Covid Meals on Wheels

This home kitchen is volunteering with freshly-prepared and nutritious vegetarian meals for patients who don’t have access to such food daily, especially senior citizens and individuals in isolation. They are also supplying to those quarantining in Covid centres. Meals include simple fare like dal, rice, roti and a sabzi, which vary each day, for instance rajma rice or chola rice. If you like, you may also sponsor a meal for someone in quarantine.
  • Meal: Lunch and dinner (both meals are delivered together)
  • Price: Free of cost
  • Delivery: The family is undertaking deliveries themselves between South Mumbai and Bandra. Beyond that, services like WeFast may be used
